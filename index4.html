Successful Transition

We must protect our students’ right to learn. Only through education can people build the skills and resources they need to best respond to crises like the one our global community is still recovering from. For some students, closures will only be a temporary shift in their schooling before they’re back on track, but for many in the communities that we serve, this could mean the end of their education.
